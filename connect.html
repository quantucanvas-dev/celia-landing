Common questions

Will my buyers notice anything different?
No. They continue messaging the same number they already know. The only difference is they now get an instant reply at any hour instead of waiting for you.
Can I still reply to buyers myself?
Yes. Whenever you reply to a buyer directly, Celia automatically detects this and goes silent for that conversation. You are always in control.
What happens to my existing WhatsApp conversations?
Your existing conversations are untouched. Celia only handles new incoming messages going forward.
Is my WhatsApp number safe?
Yes. The connection uses Meta's official Business API — the same infrastructure used by major banks and enterprises. Your number remains yours and you can disconnect at any time.
How long does setup take?
The verification step takes under 2 minutes. We complete the backend setup within a few hours and send you a confirmation on WhatsApp when Celia is live on your number.
What if I use WhatsApp Personal, not WhatsApp Business?
You will need to switch to WhatsApp Business — it is free and available on the App Store and Google Play. Your chat history and contacts are preserved when you switch. We can walk you through this if needed.
