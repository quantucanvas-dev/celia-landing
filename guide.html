Getting started

When you subscribe, Celia sends you a WhatsApp message to walk you through setup. The onboarding takes about 3 minutes and covers two things: setting your name and adding your first listing.

Celia will ask what name to use when introducing itself to buyers. If your name is David, Celia will say "I'm David's assistant" to every buyer who messages you.

After onboarding, your buyers are covered 24/7. Celia responds to every enquiry instantly — whether it's 2pm or 2am.

💡 Celia never reveals it is an AI. Buyers think they are talking to your human assistant. This is intentional — it keeps the experience personal and professional.

4

Taking over a conversation

If you want to handle a buyer conversation personally, simply reply to them directly from your WhatsApp. Celia automatically detects that you have stepped in and goes silent for 2 hours.

After 2 hours of no activity from you, Celia automatically resumes handling that conversation. Every time you send a message, the 2-hour timer resets — so as long as you keep the conversation going, Celia stays silent.

💡 You never need to type a command to take over. Just reply to the buyer naturally and Celia gets out of the way.
5

Generating listing content

Describe any property to Celia and it will generate three pieces of content instantly: a PropertyGuru listing description, an Instagram caption, and a WhatsApp broadcast message.

Example — describe the property naturally
I have a 3BR at Parc Esta, District 14, 1000sqft, asking $2.1M, 99yr leasehold, mid floor, near Eunos MRT, owner upgrading

Celia will respond with all three pieces of content, CEA-compliant and ready to use. You can ask it to adjust tone, shorten, or change any detail.

💡 All listing descriptions generated by Celia are CEA-compliant — no misleading claims, no unverified superlatives. Safe to use directly on PropertyGuru and 99.co.

7

Morning briefing

Every morning at 7:30am, Celia sends you a WhatsApp summary of your leads categorised into three groups:

HOT — messaged in the last 24 hours. These are your priority calls for the day.

WARM — last active 1 to 3 days ago. Follow up today before they go cold.

COLD — last active 3 to 7 days ago. Celia will send them an automated follow-up today.

💡 Read your briefing over morning coffee before you open your laptop. You will know exactly who to call and what to say before your day starts.

9

Co-broke detection

Every new number that messages you is automatically checked against the CEA Public Register. If the number belongs to a licensed property agent, Celia alerts you immediately instead of treating them as a buyer lead.

This prevents you from sharing your full listing brochure and floor plans with a competitor who is fishing for marketing materials.

⚠️ Celia sends a neutral opening reply to the agent while alerting you privately. You decide how to proceed with the co-broke enquiry.
